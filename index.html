About Us

Our Business

Founded in 2012; EdgeSpot Interiors is a leading provider of interior design styles. Our team combine their skills to meet the needs of the customer, focussing on providing the best in Homes and Commercial Market sectors.

We offer a complete design service for Homes/Holiday homes/Offices/Gardens. Your needs are considered with great attension providing the basis for a careful and thorough study of the project; Contracting and providing turn key solution, interior & exterior design and decor.

Our highly skilled design team gives a great attention to understand the needs and taste of each client which results in bespoke and personalised styling.

Design Philosophy

Our Design philosophy revolves around four values:

  • Asthetics
  • Functionality
  • Personality
  • Value

Our design & products are categorised by unparalleled combination of detailing, innovative use of materials and unique personalisation. All crafted with uncompromising standards of quality and excellence.
